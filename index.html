TIPS
GET READY TO CRUISE

When it's time to take the whole family on an adventure, give them one they'll never forget. Freestyle Cruising® on Norwegian Cruise Line gives you more choices of what to do and see — including exclusive Nickelodeon™ entertainment — and more freedom to do things your way. Kids can play. Parents can relax and unwind. And with no set schedules, every delicious meal and onboard discovery is up to you. On Norwegian, everyone in your family gets the vacation they’ve always dreamed of.

Check out these helpful tips and tricks that will be sure to get you and your family cruising in style:

Choose the right cruise

Experience an unforgettable family vacation on Norwegian — named one of the top large ship cruise lines for families by Travel + Leisure in 2012.

Wake up to waffles with SpongeBob and friends. Or watch your kids act out their favorite books during Story Time with Dora. Only Norwegian offers exclusive Nickelodeon™ entertainment and activities for a once-in-a-lifetime vacation experience for the whole family. Check out Nickelodeon entertainment on Norwegian Breakaway, Norwegian Epic, Norwegian Gem & Norwegian Jewel. Click here to learn more!

Check Your Documents

Once your cruise is booked, your next step is to make sure all family members’ passports and visas are valid and up-to-date. Print and bundle all reservations, itineraries, tickets and keep them safe and easily accessible.

What To Pack

Researching seasonal weather conditions and the ship’s amenities and activities will help you pack accordingly. Bring everything from bathing suits to sneakers for all the activities you will enjoy with the family!

What To Wear

When it comes to what to wear, you can go with resort casual or get all-decked out and look your best – it’s your call. That’s the freedom of Freestyle Cruising.

Organize Your Luggage

Each guest is allowed two pieces of checked luggage—if traveling by air, be sure to check your airline’s restrictions. Luggage should be clearly labeled with your name, address, and contact number.

Schedule Ahead

Make a note of the two important arrival times on your itinerary—embarkation time and departure time. Avoid rushing when traveling (while checking-in and boarding) will reduce stress for the entire family.

Now kick off your cruise vacation and enjoy the best family vacation ever!
